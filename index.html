About Us

Learn and Explore Our Facilities

Ambulance Services

At Pulse Point, our ambulance services are available 24/7 to ensure timely medical support during emergencies. We are equipped with advanced life-support systems and staffed by trained paramedics who prioritize your safety every step of the way.

Free Check-Ups

At Pulse Point, we believe that healthcare should be accessible to everyone, and that starts with prevention. That’s why we offer regular free check-up camps and basic health screenings for our community. With guidance from certified professionals, you’ll receive the care and advice needed to stay informed and empowered about your health — all at no cost.

Emergency Room

The Emergency Rooms at Pulse Point are open 24/7, fully equipped to handle critical cases with speed and precision. From accidents to sudden illnesses, our dedicated medical team ensures that every patient receives prompt attention and life-saving care.
